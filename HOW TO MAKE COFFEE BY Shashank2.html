How To Make Coffee

Ingredients :

  • Coffee Powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Steps to make coffee:

  1. TURN ON THE STOVE
  2. PUT THE UTENCIL ON THE STOVE
  3. POUR 2 CUPS OF MILK
  4. ADD 1 TEASPOON COFFEE POWDER
  5. ADD 1 SPOON SUGAR
    (you can change it according to your taste)
  6. WAIT FOR APPROX. 5 MINUTES
  7. POUR THE COFFEE IN THE CUPS
  8. ENJOY THE COFFEE
